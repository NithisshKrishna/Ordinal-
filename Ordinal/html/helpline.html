Helplines

Not until we are lost do we begin to understand ourselves.

HELPLINES IN INDIA
    Lifeline Foundation

Lifeline Foundation

Lifeline Foundation is a safe and confidential place where you can share your feelings without being judged

    Samaritans

Samaritans Mumbai

Samaritans Mumbai is a helpline providing emotional support to anyone feeling stressed, distressed, depressed, or suicidal.

    ND Prana LifeLine

ND Prana Lifeline

ND Prana Lifeline provides free, immediate support for everyone with topics including anxiety, depression, relationships

    Jeevan Aastha

Jeevan Aastha Helpline

Jeevan Aastha Helpline is for suicide prevention and mental health counseling.

    1 Life

1 Life, Crisis Support, Suicide Prevention

Anybody who is experiencing an unbearable stressful condition, dangerous suicidal thoughts, deep depression on life can call 1 Life helpline

    Vandrevela Foundation

Vandrevala Foundation crisis intervention helpline

Vandrevala Foundation crisis intervention helpline provides 24/7 free and confidential support by phone, text, and online chat.

“You are not your illness”.The WHO has defined sustainable development goals and elaborated on the impact of mental illnesses and suicide on them. The suicide rate in India in 2015 at 15.7/100,000 is higher than the regional average of 12.9 and the global average of 10.6. This statistic has only increases since then. Suicide is the leading cause of death among those aged 15-29 in India. There remains a massive unaddressed need within the population.
